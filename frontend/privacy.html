1. What we collect

  • Account data: email address, hashed password.
  • Content you upload: documents and their extracted text/embeddings.
  • Usage data: questions you ask, generated answers, timestamps.

2. How we use it

To provide the core service (authenticate you, answer questions from your documents), to maintain security (rate limiting, abuse prevention), and to operate the service (structured logs, metrics).

3. Third parties that process your data

  • Supabase — hosts our Postgres database (accounts, documents metadata, chat logs).
  • Qdrant Cloud — hosts vector embeddings of your document content.
  • Groq — processes your questions and document excerpts to generate answers.
  • Resend — sends verification and password-reset emails.
  • Render / Vercel — hosting infrastructure for the backend/frontend.

4. Your rights

You can delete any document at any time, which removes it from both the database and vector store. You can request full account deletion by contacting us. If you are in the EU/UK, you have rights under GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and portability of your data.

5. Data retention

We retain your account and documents until you delete them or close your account. Chat logs are retained to let you revisit past conversations.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
